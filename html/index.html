About

This system demonstrates and tests an online voting system using Preference Ranking and Instant Run-off.

Preference Ranking

Each voter ranks the candidates in order of preference.

Instant Run-off

Votes are counted repeatedly in a series of "run-offs" until a clear winner is found. more

This voting system has been used in states and cities in the USA since 1912. more

Advantages

Stop "Lesser Evil" Voting

In the 2016 Presidential Race, many republicans do not care for Trump, but support him to present a united front against Clinton. Likewise, many democrats abhor Clinton but know that to NOT vote for her might allow Trump to win.

Noam Chomsky says if you live in a swing state, you must "hold your nose and vote for the lesser evil". more

Allow Third-party Voting

Each voter can vote for a third-party candidate as first choice without fear of "throwing his vote away". This makes third parties viable, and gives them a chance to win against the two dominant parties.
